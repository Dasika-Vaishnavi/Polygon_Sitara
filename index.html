OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a decentralized platform for space enthusiasts.

The contribution by the your engagement with the platform is rewarded by incentives and TARA tokens on the consensus of the amount of time spent on the platform.

This is done by launching social-governance tokens [TARA tokens] once the platform and community become strong enough.

The tokens can be transferable, giving voting powers and decision-making powers on what happens in the metaverse.
